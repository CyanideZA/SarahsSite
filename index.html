Orthomolecular Protocols and Nutrition

Vitamins, Minerals and Macros

The body could fall into a state of dis-ease or injury for all sorts of reasons. Given the correct nutrition, minerals and living conditions, the body has the ability to make astounding recoveries.

Elements and Compounds

Iodine, Methylene Blue, Castor Oil and other compounds can be extremely beneficial to the body in many capacities when used appropriately.

Enemies to Health

Seed oils, processed food, alcohol, smoking, and unrelenting stress are among the biggest enemies to optimal health that we often overlook.

Water Quality

The type of water we drink makes a huge difference to our health. Investing in a good water filtration system is essential for reducing toxins like fluoride.

Exercise at Any Age

Finding exercise you enjoy is key. Lifting weights is particularly beneficial for maintaining strength and vitality throughout life.

Diet and Nutrition

What we swallow is critically important. The first question when addressing health should always be "what are you eating and drinking?"

Circadian Biology & Environment

Natural Sunlight

The effects of natural sunlight on the body and its healing abilities. How to manage and reduce Blue light toxicity for health and healing.

The Sun's Healing Abilities

The scourge of toxic sunscreens and sunglasses on our health. We are designed to be exposed to the sun - the key is not to get burned.

Environmental Toxins

Fluoride, plastic, chemicals, skin care products, and deodorants can carry significant toxic loads that affect our health.

Benefits of Environmental Change

As you make changes to your environment, you'll notice differences in your body - more vitality, better energy, and improved wellbeing.

Water and Healing

Water quality matters for healing. Drinking from glass instead of plastic and using good filtration systems makes a significant difference.

Grounding the Body

Connecting with the earth has calming effects and helps us feel connected and whole. Grounding mats and practices can mitigate EMF exposure.

Addictions

Why We Do It to Ourselves

Adult life can be challenging, and many turn to substances to self-medicate or self-soothe. Addiction knows no race, gender, or creed and can ruin lives without intervention.

Neurotransmitters' Role

Our dopamine receptors get used to the "hit" from addictive substances, creating tolerance and dependence that poisons the body and mind.

Antidotes to Addiction

The main ingredient for recovery is willingness. Various treatments, medicines, and therapies can help, especially when combined with addressing underlying trauma.

Healing Addictions

People typically only want to get better when the pain of addiction becomes worse than what they're getting out of it. Multiple approaches work best for recovery.

Importance of Addressing Addiction

Addiction often prevents people from addressing other health concerns. It's hard to focus on blood pressure or insulin resistance when consumed by addiction.

Excuses and Denial

Many don't recognize their addictive behaviors or make excuses. Only through honest self-review can people see the need for change.

Mind, Body and Soul Healing

Every Cell Hears Every Thought

With every thought we think and every word we utter, we speak life over ourselves and others. Our bodies are so finely tuned that every cell hears every thought.

Think and Speak Yourself Well

Become aware of negative self-talk and guard against negativity creeping into your life narrative. Trust yourself that you've faced difficult things before and can do it again.

Clearing Negativity and Toxic People

Remove yourself from toxic people and don't allow others rental space in your head. Find optimistic people who support and enliven you.

Clear the Clutter

We are greatly affected by our environment. Clear clutter to improve energy and thinking. Bring plants into your environment, play calming music, and ensure fresh air and natural light.
